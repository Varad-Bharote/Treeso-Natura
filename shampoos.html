Our Herbal Shampoos

Our shampoos are formulated with time-tested herbal ingredients to cleanse, nourish, and strengthen your hair naturally. Gentle enough for daily use and suitable for all hair types.

  • 🌲 Aloe & Neem – for dandruff control

    Anti-fungal neem combined with soothing aloe vera to eliminate dandruff and soothe an itchy scalp. Restores scalp health and reduces flakiness.

  • 🌺 Hibiscus & Curry Leaves – for hair growth

    Promotes hair growth and prevents hair fall. Hibiscus strengthens hair roots while curry leaves add shine and volume for thicker, healthier hair.

  • 🍋 Lemon & Tea Tree – for oily scalp

    Clarifying lemon and antiseptic tea tree oil work together to control excess oil production and refresh your scalp. Perfect for oily hair types.

  • 🌿 Shikakai & Amla – for shine and strength

    Traditional Ayurvedic ingredients that naturally cleanse while adding luster and strength to your hair. Leaves hair soft, manageable, and frizz-free.

  • 🌹 Rose & Brahmi – for smoothness

    Rose adds a delightful fragrance while brahmi conditions the scalp and hair. Creates silky smooth hair that's easy to style and manage.
